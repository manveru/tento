About

A gift of light

As suggested by O-tento,
a humble reference to the sun in Japanese,
it is Tento’s wish that these chocolates will bring
a bit of sunshine to your everyday life.

Tento was dreamt into being by
a traditional Japanese confectionery in Nikko, Nisshodo,
partly in response to modern tastes,
which have changed with the times.
Through our chocolates, we hope to deliver a different
and pleasurable way of experiencing the new era.

As such, Tento marks the start of a new beginning
for Nisshodo, with an offering that both satisfies
modern tastes but also embodies the time-honored
traditions of Japan.
Please take all the time you need to savor our chocolates.
We think you’ll be able to taste the sunshine in every one.
